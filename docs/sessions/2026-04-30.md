## Done

- **Restored 8×8 procedural flower icons** in [`PreloadScene.ts`](../../src/scenes/PreloadScene.ts) — `TEX.FlowerRed/Yellow/Blue` had been replaced with 32×32 atlas tiles (e.g. `(0, 28)` is a wheat-field tile, not an icon), so scattering them in Skill Grove produced large yellow rectangles. Re-added `makeFlower()` and `makeMushroom()` and dropped the misleading atlas cuts.
- **Cleaned the Skill Grove in-clearing scatter** in [`SkillGroveScene.ts`](../../src/scenes/SkillGroveScene.ts) — removed `TEX.Bush` from the list (bushes belong on grass, not on the dirt clearing) and tightened the rect to inside the clearing only. 24 → 18 small decorations.
- **Fixed NPC label position** in [`NPC.ts`](../../src/entities/NPC.ts) — the hardcoded `y - 22` was tuned for 32-px sprites; at the new 64-px scale it placed labels mid-torso. Computed offset now derives from `lpcFrame * charScale * 0.85 + 6` so labels always sit just above the head.
- **Replaced garbage arrow chars** in [`ShopUIScene`](../../src/scenes/ShopUIScene.ts), [`DebugUIScene`](../../src/scenes/DebugUIScene.ts) and [`CharCreateScene`](../../src/scenes/CharCreateScene.ts) — `↑↓←→` glyphs render as junk at 9-px font size in canvas. Hints now use the actual keys the game accepts (`W/S A/D`).
- **Repositioned Engineering buildings** in [`EngineeringDistrictScene.ts`](../../src/scenes/EngineeringDistrictScene.ts) — buildings were at `y=96` (off the top of the camera view); now at `cy - TILE - BUILDING_H` so the bottom touches the avenue's north sand shoulder and the entire building + NPC label fits in viewport. Dropped the redundant standalone `work.building` text — the NPC label now carries the name.
- **Bumped character scale to 1.0** in [`config.ts`](../../src/config.ts) — `charScale: 0.5 → 1.0`. Players + NPCs are now native LPC 64-px height (~2 tiles tall, Stardew-ish). Faces are readable.
- **End-to-end visual walkthrough** via the preview tools — Title, CharCreate, Town, Engineering, Skill Grove, Fishing Dock, AI/ML Lab, Full-Stack Dungeon, Shop, Settings, Quiz, Typing, Debug, Fishing UI. Catalogued and fixed.
- **Docs/memory system** — added [`docs/project.md`](../project.md), [`docs/architecture.md`](../architecture.md), and this session log. Established the "session log + project.md + chunked commits" convention.

## Decisions

- **Engineering buildings stay on stone pavers** — the user's "smooth transitions" ask was applied where it made sense (Town brick paths, Fishing Dock shore, plaza, Skill Grove clearing, Engineering avenue shoulders). Building-on-stone is the right look for a paved district; adding a grass-dirt foundation under each building would clash because there's no grass nearby.
- **Char scale = 1.0 over 0.5** — user explicitly wanted "the face." 1.0 makes faces obvious at the cost of taller characters; we offset by re-tuning NPC label position and lowering Engineering buildings.
- **Drop standalone Engineering building labels** — duplicated the NPC label info, and pinning them to the building's roof made them clip at the camera viewport top. Single label per NPC is cleaner.
