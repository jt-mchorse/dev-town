/dev/town an interactive engineering portfolio

Controls

  • WASD / ↑↓←→ move
  • E interact
  • Space / Enter advance dialog
  • Esc leave a modal
  • 1–4 answer quiz
  • N next puzzle (debug trial)

Map

  • ← Engineering — work history
  • ↑-W Dungeon — typing + bug-spot
  • ↑-E AI/ML Lab — quizzes
  • → Skill Grove — skills + projects
  • ↓ Fishing Dock — tech-pun fish

In Town

  • greeter — orientation
  • shopkeeper — cosmetics
  • news kiosk — daily HN
  • fountain — central plaza
  • mysterious stump — credit drip

Tip

  • A golden Daily Secret chest hides somewhere new every 24h.
  • ◆ = credits. Spend at the cosmetic shop.
